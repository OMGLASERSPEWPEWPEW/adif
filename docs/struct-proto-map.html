Size: EQ: 12 bytes (bit-packed). ADIF: 38 bytes (float precision). Tradeoff: ~3x larger but no bit manipulation, supports sub-integer coordinates. At 20 updates/sec for 100 entities = 76 KB/sec vs 24 KB/sec. Acceptable for modern bandwidth.
